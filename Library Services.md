### History of JRMSU

Jose Rizal Memorial State University (JRMSU) is the premier university in the province of Zamboanga del Norte. It was established to provide quality higher education to the people of the province and beyond. The university is named after the Philippine national hero, Dr. Jose Rizal, embodying his ideals of patriotism, excellence, and service.

The Katipunan Campus, one of the five JRMSU campuses, is located at National Highway, Barangay Dos, Katipunan, Zamboanga del Norte. The campus library serves as the intellectual heart of the institution, supporting the instructional, research, and extension programs of the university.

### JRMSU Library Quality Objectives

The JRMSU Library commits to:

1. Provide relevant, up-to-date, and accessible information resources and services
2. Continuously enhance collections, facilities, and technological services
3. Promote information literacy among its academic community
4. Strengthen research support and foster collaborative linkages
5. Contribute to institutional development through quality library services
6. Nurture a culture of lifelong learning among its users

## Module: Services

### Library Services Guide (17 Services)

#### 1. Library User Education
The Librarians and staff conduct formal and informal orientation for all first year and transferees every start of the school year to acquaint and assist new users regarding the Library facilities and services. In depth instruction in the use of information resources and search strategies (use of the Online Public Access Catalog [OPAC]) are also provided.

#### 2. Informal Reference Service
This is a one on one service assistance given to the library users in their information queries.

#### 3. Readers Advisory Services
This service involves helping users locate, retrieve and otherwise, gain access to information, provide easy access to information, gives assistance to users in securing information and in using library resources.

#### 4. Technical Services
This service involves the mechanical and technical processing of books and other library materials prior for circulation. The technical librarian performs:
- Checking against Purchase Request and Acknowledgment Receipts and Inventory (ARI)
- Checking against Shelf List for added copies
- Marks newly acquired/received materials with ownership stamp
- Cataloging (bibliographic description per accepted cataloguing standard)
- Classifying books per Dewey Decimal Classification (DDC) and HEGIS Classification
- Accessioning the book in the accession record
- Encoding bibliographic data in the Integrated Library System (ILS) for OPAC
- Executing mechanical applications of the materials
- Forwarding processed materials for circulation
- Disseminating list of newly processed materials

#### 5. Audio-Visual Services
AV services provided for important events and to enhance teaching and learning. Faculty may coordinate with the librarian for class viewing/listening.

#### 6. Circulation Services
Allows library users to borrow, return, renew and reserve library materials. Fines and penalties for overdue books apply. The librarian reserves the right to exclude some materials from borrowing.

#### 7. Ask-a-Librarian / #AskRIZAL
Online queries via official social media accounts (FB Messenger, IG, Twitter), email address and library webpage. Queries answered during office hours; those received after hours are responded to the next working day.

#### 8. Photo/Scan Me Service
Taking picture or scanning of selected and limited page of books using various gadgets available.

#### 9. Online Public Access Catalog (OPAC) Service
OPAC available at strategic places of the library for easy access of resources.

#### 10. Printing Service
Hard copies of researches available for a minimal fee.

#### 11. Property Counter Service
Baggage area near entrance and exit doors for depositing personal belongings.

#### 12. Selective Dissemination of Information (SDI)
Informs library users about newly acquired resources on specialized fields.

#### 13. Current Awareness Services
Latest library news, resources, events, activities, policies, future plans and achievements via social media (FB, IG, Twitter), library webpage, and information board.

#### 14. Referral Information Service (RIS)
Service for JRMSU faculty and students who wish to avail resources of other libraries with a referral issued by the Campus Librarian.

#### 15. File Transfer Service
Digitization of print materials as requested by faculty and students for easy sharing and access.

#### 16. Internet / e-Library / Free Wi-Fi Service
Internet facilities accessible to all bona fide students and faculty.

#### 17. Online Databases Service
Links to e-books, e-journals, market research databases, legal databases, online encyclopaedias, e-magazines, and e-newspapers. Log-in credentials can be requested by email.

- **Fee:** Free
